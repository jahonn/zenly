## About

Zenly is a lightweight AI-powered wellness companion designed for remote workers. Track your daily stress, chat with an AI companion, and find calm in just 5 minutes a day.

## Features

- **Daily Check-in** - Track your stress level in 1 minute
- **AI Chat** - Personalized stress support from AI
- **5-Minute Meditation** - Guided relaxation exercises
- **Trends** - Weekly and monthly stress insights
